About Us

Decision Men a.k.a Wise Men Alliance

We believe communities are the unit of change to realize improved life outcomes for boys and young men. Research shows that the collective work of community leaders, members, public and private agencies pursuing the same goals for boys and young men can lead to sustainable, place-based change.

DPF works to unleash the power of communities working together to solve problems for boys and young men, at a level to improve real life outcomes. We believe communities are the unit of change to realize improved life outcomes for boys and young men. Research shows that collective work of community leaders, members, and public and private agencies pursuing the same goals for boys and young men can lead to sustainable, place-based change.

We believe that cross-sector collaborative action is central to how communities will achieve improved outcomes for boys and young men globally.

Nonprofits are healthy and increasing scale of high-quality service offerings directed towards boys and young men from cradle to career.

Corporations commit resources to support boys and young men and create targeted hiring and retention strategies.

Philanthropy commits sustained financial resources to support boys and young men.

Residents (e.g., YOUTH) are engaged and commit time and their voices to shape priorities and initiatives.

Meet The Founder

Dagogo Pollyn

Dagogo Pollyn is a visionary humanitarian and social entrepreneur dedicated to creating positive change in communities across Nigeria and beyond. With a deep commitment to social justice and human development, he established the Dagogo Pollyn Foundation to address critical needs in education, healthcare, and community development.

Born and raised in the Niger Delta region, Dagogo witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by underprivileged communities, which fueled his passion for social impact. His leadership has guided the foundation to implement numerous programs that have transformed thousands of lives.

Beyond his philanthropic work, Dagogo is also an accomplished. His unique blend of compassion and strategic thinking continues to drive the foundation's mission forward.

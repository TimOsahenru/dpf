About Us

Our mission is to inspire, empower, and connect people to take the Gospel of Jesus Christ everywhere they go, into their world.

Wise Women Worship(aka DEISION WOMEN) seeks to empower and educate adolescent girls around the world, allowing them to achieve their full potential and transform their families, communities, and countries.

Our goal is to connect leaders, strengthen the impact of grassroots organizations, and use public awareness to drive action in support of adolescent girls’ education and empowerment. This Alliance supports and welcomes organizations that serve adolescent girls of all gender expressions and identities.

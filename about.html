About Us

Our mission is to inspire, empower, and connect people to take the Gospel of Jesus Christ everywhere they go, into their world.

We seek to build an active Christian culture where people are equipped and motivated to make change in their communities and in the places where they live, work, and play.

Around the world, we’re empowering emerging leaders to more powerfully participate by connecting them with the skills, resources, and networks to maximize their potential and take their local impact global. We’re forging pathways to essential opportunities for young people, through education, mentorship, and career development, no matter where they’re from.

We are a global center for change with programming that invites visitors, whether they’re coming from our local communities or across the globe and we encourage them to bring change to their world.

Join us — and let's bring change to our world.

Meet The Founder

Dagogo Pollyn

Dagogo Pollyn is a visionary humanitarian and social entrepreneur dedicated to creating positive change in communities across Nigeria and beyond. With a deep commitment to social justice and human development, he established the Dagogo Pollyn Foundation to address critical needs in education, healthcare, and community development.

Born and raised in the Niger Delta region, Dagogo witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by underprivileged communities, which fueled his passion for social impact. His leadership has guided the foundation to implement numerous programs that have transformed thousands of lives.

Beyond his philanthropic work, Dagogo is also an accomplished. His unique blend of compassion and strategic thinking continues to drive the foundation's mission forward.

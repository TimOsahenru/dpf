Legacy Giving

Create a Lasting Legacy Through Estate Gifts

Estate gifts allow you to make a significant impact on our mission while providing potential tax benefits for you and your heirs. By including the Dagogo Pollyn Foundation in your estate plans, you create a legacy of hope that will continue to transform lives for generations to come.

An estate gift can be as simple as adding language to your will or trust, naming the foundation as a beneficiary of your retirement plan or life insurance policy, or establishing a charitable trust. These gifts cost nothing during your lifetime but provide crucial support for our future work.

How It Works:

  • Consult with your financial or legal advisor
  • Designate the Dagogo Pollyn Foundation as a beneficiary
  • Share your plans with us (optional but appreciated)

Estate gifts may reduce the taxable portion of your estate, potentially lowering estate taxes for your heirs.

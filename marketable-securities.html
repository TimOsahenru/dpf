Maximize Your Impact with Securities

Donating appreciated publicly traded securities that you've held for longer than 12 months may allow you to:

Claim a charitable deduction for the full market value
Potentially avoid capital gains tax
Support our mission more significantly than cash donations
Please consult your personal tax advisor for advice specific to your situation.

How to Donate Securities:

  1. Contact us at dagogopollyn@gmail.com for transfer instructions
  2. Instruct your broker to initiate the transfer
  3. Include your name in the transfer details for proper acknowledgment
